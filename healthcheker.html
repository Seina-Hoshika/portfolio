Health checker

Vancouver is a country with a lot of nature and tolerance for diversity, and is popular with people from all over the world. However, many people there feel lonely and tend to suffer from mental illness. In this project, we focused on remote workers in particular and considered how we could use digital platforms to address the mental problems they face.

Client

Healthcare company

Tool

Figma / Figjam / Zoom

Role

UI designer

Time

May 13 - 23 2024 (10days)

Process

Research

Discover the problem

First, we created a map based on their hypothetical situation to find out where they might fall into mental illness. We then interviewed people who have experience working remotely to improve the reproducibility of the map and identify the causes.

Key point

Although there are many causes of mental illness, I realized that the underlying problem is that people do not notice changes in their health.

Solution Sketch

We focused on how to make them aware of their own health problems, and we came up with the idea of creating an app that would record their health status on a daily basis. We drew a sketch of how it would affect their daily lives.

Design

This app uses AI to give people feedback on their health. People wear a smartwatch and use the app to record measurable body conditions such as sleep quality and heart rate, as well as daily emotions. Based on these records, the AI gives advice on people's health.

Takeaway

After completing the app prototype, we conducted user testing to see how it worked. As a result, we got some feedback on the user interface, and realized that it would be important to have experts help us to give more practical advice to users. The next step is to develop the app with the help of experts.
