Hoshika Clinic

A project to revamp an existing website in conjunction with the launch of a new business

Hoshika Clinic

Hoshika Pediatrics and Internal Medicine Family Clinic (hereafter Hoshika clinic) is a private clinic that is well-known among locals. In order to rebuild its finances after the COVID-19 pandemic, Hoshika Clinic has decided to revamp its business, including the services it provides. In conjunction with this, the clinic has decided to update its website The CMS they had been using previously, which was specialized for medical institutions, had many restrictions and did not allow for free customization, so they wanted to create our homepage using a CMS that offers more freedom and that we could update ourselves.

Key point

Although there are many causes of mental illness, I realized that the underlying problem is that people do not notice changes in their health.

Problem statement

The old website had some visual issues, most notably issues with hierarchy and color. Due to limitations of the server we were using previously, we were unable to adjust the overall balance, and there were many inconsistencies in alignment and text size. Regarding color, all kinds of colors were used everywhere, giving the impression that the information was overwhelming.

Key point

Create brand image

Organize information

Site map

The main menu was arranged in order of the most popular content for users, and grouped by related items. Each of the new business items, which are elective medical treatments, was created on its own page, allowing for accurate information to be delivered to specific users.
