Creating mobile app for unique apparel shop with teammates in Hackathon

Agora Arts

Agora Arts is venturing into the realm of online fashion shopping, addressing challenges faced by both users and artists in the fashion industry. On the user side, navigating through the endless scroll of online shopping can be overwhelming, making it difficult for them to find their unique style. Artists, on the other hand, often struggle to stand out on these platforms.

Client

Agora Art

Tool

UI designer

Process

Our approach

focusing on two key aspects: "User-friendly discovery" and "Highlighting the artist”.For the former, we chose familiar colours from their existing brand colours, taking into consideration their contrast ratio. For the latter, we took into consideration the information hierarchy and decided to display information in order of priority.

Information Architecture

There are three major elements of the home page. Home page is the default page of the app, setting the stage for user's journey within the app. From here, there are two other primary elements that branch out: the Event page and the Style page. In terms of the Home section, it acts as the web application's entrance, prioritizing information about Agora's new project. Agora Art is developing a feature to provide personalized recommendations on styles leveraged by AI. With this consideration, the recommendations from this feature are displayed at the top part of the home page. The second important piece of information is about new artists joining the platform. Following that, we've incorporated an inspiration style section, allowing customers to explore products based on their unique style.  These features encourage frequent visits to the site. The final element is the Events section, showcasing ongoing projects at Agora

Wireframe

The client's app was designed to be visually appealing, using a lot of photography to present fashion.By using familiar carousels and cards, the design is more intuitive and user-friendly.

Colour & Typography

Colour : Chose based on client's colour palette which has high contrast and user engagement. Typography : both are readable and have variety of thickness.

Accessibility & Interactive

We used high contrast colours to not only impress the brand image but also make the content easy to read.We also created an interactive design that would let users immediately notice when they took an action.
